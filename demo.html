Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with festive focaccia appetizer with sweet caramelized onion, tart cramberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favourite holiday starter

Ingredients

For the focaccia:
  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flow
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups(350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon oliv e oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cramberries
